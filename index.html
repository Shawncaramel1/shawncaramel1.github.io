How to Build a Blog on GitHub Pages

Published: June 1, 2026

GitHub Pages is an amazing free platform to host your personal blog or portfolio. In this post, I'll walk you through the basics and why I chose it for my creative projects.

The beauty of GitHub Pages is its simplicity. You don't need to worry about server management, hosting fees, or complicated deployments. Just push your code to GitHub, and your site is live in seconds!

Whether you're a developer, designer, or writer, GitHub Pages gives you a free, fast, and reliable way to share your work with the world. And with AdSense integration, you can even monetize your content!

The Importance of Consistent Content Creation

Published: May 25, 2026

One of the biggest lessons I've learned is that consistency beats perfection every single time. It's better to publish good content regularly than to spend months perfecting one post.

Your audience appreciates knowing they can count on you to show up regularly. Set a publishing schedule that works for you—whether that's once a week or twice a month—and stick to it.
